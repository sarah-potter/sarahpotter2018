Digital Design

With design, there’s always many avenues to take; but no matter your style, the goal remains the same: Create concepts that inspire, inform and captivate. Together we’ll establish an aesthetic and brand strategy which I’ll execute flawlessly across print, digital, or any other medium you can think of.

Development

Design can look perfect on the mockup, but in order to make it look the same across all popular browsers and devices you need to have a holistic persepcitve. I develop and deploy hand-coded websites with rapid turnaround times, all while maintaining high-standards for the experience, and having an eagle’s eye when it comes to the details.

Animation

Watch your content come to life with animations. Take your website to the next level with elements of delight and interactivity. Using my expertise across user experience and interface design, let’s make something with a wow-factor.

UI / Wireframes

In order to design original, useful and elegant digital experiences, you must have a plan. I’ll help you get from concept through execution, all while balancing user experience and the needs of business.

Lettering

After getting acquainted with nib & ink calligraphy, and then onto brush lettering, and eventually taking my skills digital with the iPad Pro, when it comes to illustrating type, I can’t stop, won’t stop. I address envelopes and create bespoke signage using classic ink, watercolor, and lots and lots of chalk.

Agritourism

About Sarah

I am an experienced designer & developer based out of Seattle, Washington. I partner with businesses big & small, and teams far & wide, to create beautiful, enticing, and fully functioning websites and experiences. From high-fidelity layouts, to clean, well-commented code – I can elegantly take your project form inception to launch.

My background also includes experiences in marketing, tech-support, customer service, and even agriculture. I’m a Cal Poly, SLO alumna, a former Apple employee & I grew up on a farm.
